# EarCrate cephalopod song reader

EarCrate reads before it composes.  The song reader is a distributed nervous
system over one exact, frame-indexed PCM body:

- the pulse arm proposes the heartbeat and non-trivial phrase cycle;
- the layer arm creates complementary physical views that sum back to the body;
- the recurrence arm groups beat-aligned audible cells into canonical events and
  exact instances;
- the residual arm names what recurrence cannot explain instead of inventing
  notes;
- the project authority stores every observation, alternative, accepted event,
  human lock, and execution receipt.

Personas configure attention and abstention.  They cannot change the waveform.

## First proof

`earcrate reader read` emits two deliberately different renders:

1. `RECURRENCE_LEAVE_ONE_OUT.wav` executes every recurrent cell from a *different*
   occurrence.  Unique cells remain silent.  This proves that the recurrence map,
   not same-time pass-through, carries the musical structure.
2. `SONG_GENOME_DIAGNOSTIC.wav` adds one explicitly reference-derived unique
   foreground residual so a one-time rap/hook entrance is audible.  It is a
   diagnostic teacher artifact and is never publication-eligible.

The proof is accepted only when raw onset and timbre correspondence pass before
reference-conditioned mastering.  No MIDI cue or symbolic annotation contributes
PCM.  No chord, sustain, or note is invented to fill uncertainty.

## Authority boundary

The reference teaches and judges.  Final publication still requires independently
eligible SourcePhrases or sealed racks.  A diagnostic SongGenome can prove that
EarCrate read the song without pretending that the answer-key audio is a licensed
ingredient.
